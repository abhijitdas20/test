ABOUT SOFTWARE WORLD

Since its inception in 2015, Software World solution private limited has grown phenomenally from a specialist in software development and support into an end-to-end IT solutions provider for Government of Tripura. Software World is primarily focused on e-governance projects in Tripura that are growing at a triple-digit growth rate every year and off-shoring solutions ranging from strategy consulting, business analysis, project management, software development, software implementation, maintenance, and support.

Software World team comprises of over 10 diverse professionals and management team who bring a strong history of domestic and international technical expertise for delivering right solutions to the client. Our team works on the reduction of operation costs allowing customers to focus on their strategies and core competencies.
